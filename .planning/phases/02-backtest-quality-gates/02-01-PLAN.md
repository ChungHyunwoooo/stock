    - Entry price adjusted by slippage: entry_price = close * (1 + slippage_pct) for buy
    - Exit price adjusted by slippage: exit_price = close * (1 - slippage_pct) for sell
    - Fee deducted from capital on both entry and exit
    - BacktestRunner() with no arguments defaults to NoSlippage + fee_rate=0.0 (backward compatible)
  </behavior>
  <action>
    1. `engine/backtest/runner.py` 수정:
